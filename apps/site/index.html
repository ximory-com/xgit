FAQ

需要服务器吗？Do I need a server?
不需要，全部前端完成，直接对接 GitHub API。No. Everything runs in the browser with GitHub API.
支持私有仓库吗？Private repos?
支持，只需授予 Token（建议 scope: repo）。Yes. Grant a token (scope: repo) and you’re good.
移动端体验如何？Mobile experience?
采用单屏栈式导航，操作更顺手。Single-screen stack navigation for smooth operations.
什么是 ZIP 协议？ What is the ZIP Protocol?
XGit 提出的轻量协作通道：把目录与资源一次打包（ZIP）进行交互，保证上下文完整；后续支持“增量 ZIP”（仅含变化文件） 与快照式留存，特别适合与 AI 协作（人类打包 → AI 理解输出 ZIP → 人类验证）。 A lightweight collaboration channel by XGit: exchange folders as ZIP to preserve full context. Incremental ZIP (changed files only) and snapshot history are planned — ideal for AI workflows (human package → AI produce ZIP → human verify).
为什么比复制粘贴更高效？ Why is it better than copy & paste?
复制粘贴容易零碎且丢上下文；ZIP 协议把目录结构、静态资源与配置一次性带上， 一次传输、一解即用，减少来回对齐与遗漏，并天然形成“版本快照”，便于回退与对比。 Copy-paste is fragmented and loses context. The ZIP Protocol ships structure, assets and configs together: one transfer, one unzip, ready to run. It naturally creates “snapshots” for rollbacks and diffing.
